MANAGEMENT

CHARL DE VILLIERS

CEO (DIRECTOR)

Charl de Villiers is the CEO of X-Store. Charl has more than 10 years corporate marketing experience with companies such as Distell and Saambou Bank and holds a B.Tech Marketing degree from the Pretoria Technikon.

He also did Marketing Consultation for various SMME’s, including the X-Store Group, Cadbury, Minanawe Advertising and Stannic Fleet Management and was a part-time lecturer in marketing at Damelin Education Group from 2001 to 2004.

Charl joined X-Store in June 2004 when the company was repositioning itself and required the expertise of a marketing expert. Charl was born in Somerset West in the Western Cape. He is passionate about nature conservation, loves animals and enjoys a good red wine or whisky. Charl has a diverse taste in music and practice photography as a hobby.

LOURENS BOSHOFF

CHIEF INFORMATION OFFICER (CIO)

Since I always had a passion for computers and technology, I studied Computer Science at the University of Pretoria where I completed my BSc (Hons) degree in Computer Science in 2004.

I started at Suremax as Junior Programmer in March 2005, from where I was promoted, in time, first to Senior Programming Analyst and finally to my current position as Product Development Manager. It is my job to oversee the in-house Software Development Life Cycle of X-Store and to look after our client’s needs on a software level. My team and I strive to give the most generic and functional software possible.

I love the challenges that go with software development and the problem solving that is required to achieve the best solutions for our valued customers. My other passions include rugby (Go Bulls!), fly fishing and computer gaming.

LYDIA BADENHORST

CHIEF HUMAN RESOURCE OFFICER (CHRO)

Lydia Badenhorst (de Villiers) was born 19 February 1968 in Ladybrand. She matriculated in Pretoria, Oos-Moot High School in 1985, obtained her Executive Secretary Diploma in 1988 at Technicon Pretoria and HR Diploma 2008 at Varsity College.

She truly believes that achieving your dreams, is the most awesome fuel for the soul! Never before has she felt so good about life. With an increased workload and much more responsibility, she strangely feels calmer, more in control.

She is forced on a daily basis to give gratitude for her good health and shared successes. I believe the key ingredients for success are happiness, inspiration & motivation. So there you have it. What you make of your attitude, happiness and life is up to you.
