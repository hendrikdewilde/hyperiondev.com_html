TOM CLANCYS THE DIVISION - R495.00

A devastating pandemic sweeps through New York City causing basic services to rapidly fail one by one. Society collapses into total chaos in only a matter of days as essential needs such as food and water become scarce.

The President invokes Presidential Directive 51, and The Division, a classified unit of self-supported tactical agents, is activated during this emergency scenario. Leading seemingly ordinary lives among us, Division agents are trained to operate independently in order to save society. When society falls, your mission begins.

Powered by our new game engine SnowdropTM, tailor-made for next-gen consoles, Tom Clancy's The Division achieves a whole new level of gameplay quality. Snowdrop takes full advantage of the new consoles with dynamic global illumination, stunning procedural destruction and an insane amount of detail and visual effects.
